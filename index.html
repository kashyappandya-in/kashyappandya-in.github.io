The Man Behind the Architecture

I'm an infrastructure and automation leader with two decades of experience building and modernising enterprise IT across some demanding environments — government transport infrastructure in Belgium, mission-critical database programs for a major government bank in the Middle East, and automation work for financial services in Singapore.

Earlier in my career, I led a database tower team handling 24/7 shared services for Indian clients across BFSI, Oil & Gas, and TTH sectors — where uptime wasn't negotiable and the team had to deliver regardless. Before that, at IBM, I travelled across the APAC region — Singapore, South Korea, Bangkok, Manila — designing and implementing data security frameworks using IBM Guardium and Optim, and running hands-on client workshops on secure database management. That international exposure, combined with on-ground delivery experience, laid the foundation for the broader transformation mandate I took on at TCS.

That foundation — from hands-on DBA work through to enterprise-wide cloud transformation — is what allows me to lead programs that are technically credible and commercially grounded at the same time.
